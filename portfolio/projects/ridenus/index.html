"Pick and ride"
Is there a better way to facilitate students traveling in campus? That's the problem this project is aiming to solve. The lack of flexibility of exisiting shuttle bus system is causing much inefficiency.

A good way we found out, through a series of user studies, is that an electrical scooter rental system could meet the requirement. Thus, we developed such a system that people could pick up scooters easily around NUS campus.

An iOS App, as introduced here, is designed to be used together with the rental system.
Find your ride
Scooters are store at designated charging stations.

Find the nearest charging station and book your own ride. Each scooter will be reserved for you for 15 minutes.

We introduce a map view as the main interface for locating user location and marking available scooter stations.

Station information would drop down upon click, and user could book their ride for their trip.
Scan & Unlock
To unlock the bike, simply scan the QR code on the front bar.

User could unlock a scooter even if they did not booked a ride.

A flashlight button is provided here for quick lighting access.
Slide & Return
When finish using the bike, just slide it back to the chargin seat, and the user is good to go.
Find your way
Navigation is available while the user is on its ride.

User could find their destination by either directly moving around on the map, or type to search for a specific location on campus.

Routes will be highlighted for the user when navigation starts.
Profile & Records
User profiles are associated with their NUS account.

Here the user could either manage their payment methods, or review their past trips.
