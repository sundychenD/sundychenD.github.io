Project Description


Worked as an operation technolgy analyst, I was assigned to design the site for on-boarding enterprise clients.

The site tracks the on-boarding process of Goldman Sachs regional clients, it shows the overall boarding progress of an prospective client, reports any error or potential risk occurs during the process.

The site is built for client-on-boarding team for continuous monitoring of on-boarding process.
